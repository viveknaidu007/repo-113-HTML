Konidela Pawan Kalyan (born Konidela Kalyan Babu; 2 September[n 1]) is an Indian actor, filmmaker, politician and founder of the Jana Sena Party. Primarily working in Telugu cinema, he is known for his unique acting style and mannerism. He has a large fan base and a cult following and is one of the highest-paid actors of Indian cinema. He has been featured in Forbes India's Celebrity 100 list multiple times since 2013. Kalyan is the recipient of a Filmfare Award South and a SIIMA Award among other accolades.[5]
